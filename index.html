WHAT IS R-NPSJRMUN

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents the first edition of R-NPSJRMUN!

An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented.

Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will help sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!

    LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

    Dear Delegates,

        As Secretary-General of R-NPSMUN 2023: The 7th Edition, I am thrilled to extend my warmest greetings to each and every one of you. Going from a shy 8th grader to being the Sec Gen of a MUN has been one of the most fulfilling and validating experiences of my life. That is what I hope MUN will become for you, it is more than fiery debates and intensive research, it is the ability to make lifelong connections and unforgettable memories. Whether it is your first time attending a MUN or your tenth, I promise R-NPSMUN 2023 will be one to remember, our top-notch Executive Board and dedicated Secretariat have done everything they can to ensure a smooth conference for you all. So I urge you, my dear delegates, to seize this opportunity, immerse yourselves in the world of diplomacy, and leave no stone unturned in your pursuit of excellence. I have immense faith in your ability to take R-NPSMUN to its pinnacle.

    Get ready to join the madness, embrace the chaos, and let your inner diplomat shine. Remember, it's not just about winning awards and accolades. It's about showcasing your eloquence while managing to keep a straight face and mastering the art of diplomatic charm with a smile. Please feel free to reach out to me for anything, before or during the conference.

    May the odds be ever in your favour!

    -Dhruti Kedilaya

    LETTER FROM THE DIRECTOR GENERAL

    Greetings, Delegates!

       Welcome to the R-NPSMUN 2023, the seventh session. Like always, we aspire to be even greater than before, and this year's MUN is set to rise to greater heights than ever before. Our Executive Board has worked tirelessly to present five extraordinary committees, each with its own captivating agenda. It won't matter if you are a first-timer or a veteran, R-NPSMUN will be a conference you will never forget. Even though my first MUN was years ago, it took a lot of time for me to completely understand what people meant when they said, “Once a person has been to a MUN, it changes them forever.” You’re exposed to a completely different way of communication that does not come naturally. You’re exposed to new people with whom you have to interact with, convince, and pitch your own agendas. MUNing forces you to think outside your comfort zone and advocate for topics that you yourself might not agree with. Once you come out of that protective shell, you will become the best possible version of yourself.

    So dive into debates, speeches, crises, and press conferences. Remember, MUNing isn't about winning, but rather about making memories and friends. Above all, when you find yourself surrounded by delegates dressed in formals, trying to maintain a professional and serious environment, you will find yourself holding back a smile of enjoyment. And that smile is what makes MUNs great.

    May the odds be ever in your favour!

    -Anirudh M
